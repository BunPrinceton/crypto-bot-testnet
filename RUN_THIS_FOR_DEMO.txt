STEP 1: Start the Dashboard
----------------------------
Run this command in your terminal:

    ./run_dashboard.sh

OR if that doesn't work:

    cd src && python3 dashboard.py


STEP 2: Open Your Browser
--------------------------
Visit this URL:

    http://localhost:5000


STEP 3: Wait for Data
----------------------
- First update takes 10-15 seconds
- Watch the "Iteration" counter increment
- Dashboard auto-refreshes every 10 seconds


WHAT YOU'LL SEE:
================

Left Panel:   Live prices from Binance, Kraken, Coinbase
Right Panel:  Arbitrage opportunities (if any exist)
Bottom Panel: Market statistics and metrics
